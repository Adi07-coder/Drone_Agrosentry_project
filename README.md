#  AgroSentry

##  Introduction

### Problem Statement

Agriculture remains the backbone of many economies, yet traditional farming practices rely heavily on manual crop inspection and blanket pesticide spraying. These methods are time-consuming, labor-intensive, and often fail to detect diseases at an early stage, resulting in reduced crop yield, increased costs, and environmental damage.

### Our Solution

**AgroSentry** is an AI-powered autonomous agricultural drone designed to revolutionize precision farming. The system combines drone technology, computer vision, artificial intelligence, and targeted spraying mechanisms to monitor crop health, detect plant diseases in real time, and automatically spray pesticides or fertilizers only where required.

By enabling early disease detection and precision agriculture, AgroSentry helps farmers increase productivity, reduce chemical wastage, lower operational costs, and promote sustainable farming practices.

# Project Overview

AgroSentry is an autonomous drone ecosystem capable of:

* Real-time crop monitoring
* AI-based plant disease detection
* Autonomous field navigation
* Precision pesticide spraying
* Smart agricultural analytics
* Sustainable farm management

The drone captures field images during flight and processes them using computer vision models. Once a disease is identified, the system marks the affected region and performs targeted spraying only where needed.

#  Methodology

## Step 1: Autonomous Flight

Drone follows predefined GPS coordinates covering the entire field.

## Step 2: Image Acquisition

High-resolution images of crops are captured continuously.

## Step 3: AI-Based Disease Detection

Computer vision algorithms analyze crop images and identify infected regions.

## Step 4: Decision Making

The system determines whether spraying is required.

## Step 5: Precision Spraying

Only affected crops receive pesticides or fertilizers.

## Step 6: Report Generation

Health reports and analytics are generated for farmers.
